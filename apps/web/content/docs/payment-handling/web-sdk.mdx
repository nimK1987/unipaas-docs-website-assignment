---
title: Web SDK
description: Embed secure card fields directly in your own checkout.
---

The embedded **Web SDK** keeps shoppers on your page while Unipaas hosts the
secure card fields, so you stay out of PCI scope without sending shoppers away.

## How it works

1. Your server creates a checkout and receives a `sessionToken`.
2. You pass the `sessionToken` to the browser.
3. The Web SDK renders secure, Unipaas-hosted card fields inside your page.
4. The shopper pays without ever leaving your site.

<Callout title="Use the sessionToken, not your private key">
  The browser only ever sees the short-lived `sessionToken`. Your private key
  stays on the server.
</Callout>
